About Us

We are a team Sigma developers, engineers, and AI experts from Yeshwantrao Chavan College of Engineering,Nagpur Computer Technology Department who created Codastra to automate rote parts of serverless development and make backend coding faster and easier. Our service allows you to generate Lambda functions simply by describing what you want in plain English - no manual coding required. Codastra handles the boilerplate work so you can focus on your app's unique logic. Our AI models are continually trained to keep improving the quality of generated code. Together, our team's expertise in AI, full-stack development, machine learning, and design enables us to create an intuitive platform that boosts productivity for both expert and beginner developers. Our mission is to transform serverless development into a creative rather than repetitive process. We're excited for you to try Codastra and see how much time it saves you in building backend code and APIs!
